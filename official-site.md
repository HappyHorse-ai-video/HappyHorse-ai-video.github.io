## The current public access path

If your immediate goal is to try the product surface, the clearest path this site can point to right now is [happy-horse.net](https://www.happy-horse.net). That is why it is surfaced prominently throughout this GitHub Pages site.

## How to think about “official”

The phrase **official site** carries more weight than “current place to try it.” Before using that label confidently, it helps to check for:

- legal or company identity pages,
- stable product documentation,
- consistent branding across channels,
- clear pricing or terms,
- and developer or support navigation that matches the product claims.

## Why this page is still useful

An honest official-site page does two jobs:

1. It sends users to the best visible access point right now.
2. It gives them a framework for deciding how much trust to assign to that destination.

That is better than either extreme: saying nothing, or asserting certainty that the public evidence does not yet support.
